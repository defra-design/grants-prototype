Who can apply

You can apply if you:

  • grow crops for the food industry, are a nursery growing ornamentals or are a forestry nursery
  • will do the grant-funded work in England
  • an estimate the project costs will be over £87,500

Before you apply

Read the latest guidance on the water management grant to learn about our grant funding priorities.

What you'll need:

You'll need to give us:

  • information about your business (for example, number of employees and turnover)
  • information about the project (for example, irrigated land in hectares)
  • estimated total cost of the items you need
