Will the roof of the grant-funded housing be able to support solar PV panels?

You must provide confirmation from a building expert, contractor or structural engineer that your roof can support solar PV panels for your full application. Confirmation is not funded by this grant.

Your roof is exempt if:

  • the building is listed or on a World Heritage Site
  • you're upgrading an existing building and would not otherwise make changes to the roof
  • the roof faces only north or is heavily shaded
  • the roof does not have 100m2 of clear roof space

Eligibility

The roof must be able to support solar PV panels, allowing for potential use in the future.
