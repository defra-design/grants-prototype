Use this service to check:

  • if you are eligible to apply for a grant for your project (takes about 15 minutes)
  • how your project scores against the grant funding priorities (takes about 15 minutes if you have all the project details)

Who can apply

You can apply if you:

  • are the registered keeper of 1,000 or more laying hens or pullets
  • will do the grant-funded work in England

What you can apply for

You can apply for up to 40% of the estimated project costs of either:

  • refurbishing or replacing existing laying hen or pullet housing
  • adding a veranda to existing laying hen or pullet housing

If you are replacing or refurbishing a laying hen building, you must add a veranda if you enough outside space. This must be at least either:

  • 4 metres wide along the length of the hen housing area
  • 30% of the indoor hen housing area footprint

If you are applying for building project grant funding, you can apply for up to 25% of the estimated costs of buying and installing a solar PV system which produces up to 5 kilowatts (kW) of power capacity per 1,000 birds.

You can apply for grant funding for multiple projects. You must submit an application for each project. You cannot apply for grant funding for both building and veranda-only projects.

The maximum total grant amount each business can apply for is £500,000 for building projects (including solar PV system costs) or £100,000 for veranda-only projects. The Rural Payments Agency (RPA) will award the veranda-only grant funding on a first come, first served basis.

For building projects, the minimum grant is £15,000 (40% of £37,500). For veranda-only projects, the minimum grant is £5,000 (40% of £12,500).

Before you start

To use the checker, you need:

  • information about the project (for example, planning permission requirements, insulation type, ventilation system capacity, multi-tier system information, veranda specifications)
  • an estimate of the total cost of the project
  • information about your business (for example, county parish holding (CPH) number, number of employees, turnover)

If you do not enter any information for more than 20 minutes, your application will time out and you will have to start again.

Problems using the online service

If you have any problems using the online service, call the RPA and follow the options for the Farming Transformation Fund scheme.
