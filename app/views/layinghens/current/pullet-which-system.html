Which multi-tier system will the pullet housing have?

A system that houses birds within tiers during the first 2 weeks of rearing and enables them to access the barn floor after 2 weeks
A floor system that resembles a rearing aviary post-brooding, with adjustable elevated tiers, which can be added and gradually raised as the birds grow.

Eligibility

The pullet housing must have a multi-tier system to enable the birds to transition between levels without flying or jumping more than 1m in height, horizontally or vertically.
