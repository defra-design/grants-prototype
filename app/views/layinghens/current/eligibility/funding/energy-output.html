How much energy will your solar PV system produce?

This grant will fund a solar PV system that produces up to 5kW kilowatts (kW) of energy per 1,000 birds.

You can install a solar PV system that produces more than 5kW of energy per 1,000 birds. You must fund the extra solar PV system costs above the output capacity of 5kW per 1,000 birds.
