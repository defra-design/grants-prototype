You cannot apply for a grant from this scheme

The housing's lighting system must have:

  • non-flicker LED light with a colour temperature between 2700 and 4000 Kelvin  
  • capacity for zonal dimming between 0 and 60 lux 
  • coverage of the entire floor-litter (scratch) area 
  • a simulated stepped dawn and dusk (unless this is already provided as part of an aviary lighting system) 
  • an option for red light 

See other grants you may be eligible for.
