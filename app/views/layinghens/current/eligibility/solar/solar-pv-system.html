You can choose to buy a solar PV system that provides power to this building as part of the grant. You must install the solar PV panels on the roof of this building.

A solar PV system can include:

  • an electrical grid connection (your housing must have an existing energy supply)
  • solar panels
  • an inverter
  • a utility meter
  • a battery
