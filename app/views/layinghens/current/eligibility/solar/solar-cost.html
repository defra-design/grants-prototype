Estimated solar PV system cost and floorspace size

There is no minimum funding amount for buying and installing a  solar PV system. 

Funding for a solar PV system will be capped at the lowest of these 2 amounts, either:

  • the cost of 75m2 of the bird floorspace or
  • 25% of the invoice price for purchase and installation of the solar PV system that you will need to submit at full application

Do not include VAT
