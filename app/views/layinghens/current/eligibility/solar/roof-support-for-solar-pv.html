Eligibility

The roof must be able to support solar PV panels, allowing for potential use in the future.

Your roof is exempt if:

  • the building is listed
  • the building is on a World Heritage Site
  • you're upgrading an existing building and would not otherwise make changes to the roof
  • the roof faces only north
  • the roof is heavily shaded
  • the roof does not have 100m2 of clear roof space
