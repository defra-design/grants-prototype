Will the veranda have these features?

The veranda of the housing must have:

  • a solid concrete floor
  • a waterproof insulated roof
  • guttering and a down-pipe to feed into the drainage system of the main building
  • a perimeter wall at least 1 metre high with lockable pop holes, unless the veranda forms part of an indoor barn system
  • mesh roller-screen system running underneath the length of the roof, that fits securely against the wall when extended
  • a dimmable LED lighting system between 0 lux and 60 lux
