Will the aviary system have ramps or platforms that meet manufacture recommendations for high welfare?

The frequency and position of ramps and platforms must meet the manufacturer's recommendation for a high welfare system.

Eligibility

Unless the aviary system has been designed for easy access it must include welfare ramps or platforms to allow the birds free movement around and within the system.

The birds must be able to move between levels without flying or jumping more than one metre in height.
