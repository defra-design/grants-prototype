Will the ventilation have these features? 

The ventilation must have:

  • a control system to automatically measure and record the daily temperature, humidity and air levels
  • an alarm system (that detects excessive high or low temperatures and system failures) with a power supply independent of mains electricity
  • an emergency power supply in case of electrical or other failure, for example a high-capacity generator
