Will the hen housing have a secure cleaning area at each external pedestrian access point?

At each external pedestrian access point, the building must have:

  • hand washing facilities that are externall to the cleaning area
  • a floor-mounted barrier separating the cleaning area from the bird living area
  • a changing area with separate storage for clean and dirty boots or clothing

  • a floor-mounted barrier
