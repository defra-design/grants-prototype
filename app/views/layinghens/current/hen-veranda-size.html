Will the housing have a veranda that is at least the same size as 30% of the indoor bird housing area footprint?

Barn system projects that do not have access to an area at least 30% the size of the indoor housing area are exempt from adding a veranda

or

Eligibility

You must add a veranda that is at least the same size as 30% the indoor bird housing area footprint.

Barn system projects that do not have access to an external area at least 30% of the indoor housing area are exempt.

You must exclude the veranda's floor area from calculations of stocking density (even if providing 24 hour access).
