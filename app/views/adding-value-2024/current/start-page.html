Check if you can apply for a Farming Transformation Fund Adding Value Grant

Use this service to check:

  • if you are eligible to apply for a grant for your project (takes about 5 minutes)
  • how well your project scores against the funding priorities (takes about 15 minutes if you have all the project details)

Who can apply

You can apply if you either:

  • are a grower or producer of agricultural or horticultural produce
  • are a business processing agricultural or horticultural products that is at least 50% owned by agricultural or horticultural producers
  • produce wild venison products as part of woodland management

You can apply for up to 40% of the estimated cost of project items. You have the option to apply for up to 25% of the estimated cost of buying and installing a solar PV system.

The minimum grant for project costs is £25,000 (40% of £62,500). The maximum grant you can apply for is £300,000.

You can apply for up to an additional £100,000 for solar PV systems costs. Funding for solar PV cannot be more than grant funding for project costs.

Before you start

To use the checker, you need:

  • information about your business (for example, number of employees, turnover)
  • information about the project (for example, the types of products being processed and how you will add value, the impact the project will have)
  • a list of the items you'd like to buy for the project
  • an estimate of the total cost of the items
