The Farming Investment Fund (FIF) provides grants to improve productivity and bring environmental benefits. FIF is made up of 2 separate funds:

  • Farming Equipment and Technology Fund (for grants between £2,000 and £25,000)
  • Farming Transformation Fund (for grants between £35,000 and £500,000)

Farming Equipment and Technology Fund

This fund provides grants towards the cost of equipment and technology to improve the productivity of farms in a sustainable way.

How to apply

Round 1 applications open on 1 March 2022 and close at 31 May 2022. Read the FETF Round 1 manual for full details about who is eligible and what you can get funding for before you apply.

Apply now for an FETF grant

If you have any questions

Email FETFEnquiries@rpa.gov.uk or call the RPA helpline on 03000 200 301 and select the option for the Farming Equipment & Technology Fund.

Farming Transformation Fund

This fund provides grants towards large capital items to help businesses improve productivity, profitability, and environmental sustainability. They are:

The minimum grant you can claim is £35,000 (40% of £87,500). The maximum grant is £500,000.

  • Water Management
  • Improving Farm Productivity
  • Adding Value

What happens next

RPA will assess the information you submit. If your project is suitable, you will be asked to submit a full application.

You should read the Farming Transformation Fund Improving Farm Productivity grant manual before you submit an application.


If you have any questions

Email FTF@rpa.gov.uk or call RPA on 03000 200301 and select the option for the Farming Transformation Fund.
