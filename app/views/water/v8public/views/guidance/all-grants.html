Rural grants and payments claims

Make or update a rural payments claim in England
Apply for a rural grant or payment, including the Basic Payment Scheme (BPS), or update your claim details.

Open for applications

Basic Payment Scheme
For farmers with at least 5 hectares of land.

Countryside Stewardship
Funding for farmers, woodland owners, foresters and land managers to help them look after and improve the environment.

Environmental Stewardship
Funding for farmers and land managers who manage their land in a way that protects and helps the environment and wildlife.

Grants for equipment to improve productivity
Up to £12,000 to help farmers buy specific equipment.

Grants for water resource management projects
Up to £1 million towards reservoirs and irrigation projects.

Grants for slurry equipment
Up to £1 million towards specific equipment that makes more efficient use of slurry, manure and digestate.

RDPE Growth Programme
Grants for projects that create jobs and growth in the rural economy.

Closed for applications

England Coast Path Establishment Fund (ECPEF)
Grants to prepare areas for public use for land owners, managers, and access authorities

Farming Ammonia Reduction Grant Scheme
To help farmers install slurry store and lagoon covers.

RDPE LEADER funding
Localised funding for projects that create jobs and benefit the rural economy

Water Environment Grant (WEG)
To help organisations and land managers improve the water environment
