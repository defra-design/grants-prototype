Small grants available from DD MONTH YYYY to DD MONTH YYYY.

Who can apply

These grants are for:

  • livestock, dairy, arable and horticultural farmers
  • agents on behalf of farmers
  • farmers who also do some contracting work
  • solely contracting businesses (including contractors without a farm)
  • new agricultural businesses

How to apply for a small grant

  1. Register for the Rural Payments Service.

If you are already registered, sign in and check that the details held are up-to-date and correct

  1. Choose the items you need to improve productivity.
  2. Apply for a countryside productivity grant (opens in a new window or tab)

The Rural payments agency will assess the application against others, consider how well it fits the priorities for funding and if it provides the best value for taxpayers’ money.

  1. If the application is successful, you will be emailed a Grant Funding Agreement.
  2. Accept the grant offer using the acceptance portal by midnight DD MMMM YYYY
  3. Buy all the capital items as detailed in your agreement.
  4. Email CPSGClaims@rpa.gov.uk to claim your grant payment before midnight DD MMMM YYYY.
  5. If your claim is successful, we will pay the grant money into the bank account registered with the Rural Payments service.
