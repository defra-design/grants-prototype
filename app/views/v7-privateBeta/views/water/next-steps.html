Next steps

1 Submit your details

Submit your details to the RPA (you should only do this once).
You’ll get an email with your score and answers, and a reference
number.


2 RPA will email you when applications open

RPA will be in touch when the full application period opens.
They’ll tell you about the application form and any guidance
you need to submit a full application.


! You must not start the project

Starting the project or committing to any costs (such as
placing orders) before you receive a funding agreement
will invalidate your application.

Before you start the project, you can:

  • get quotes from suppliers
  • apply for planning permission or an abstraction licence

You will not automatically get a grant. The grant is
expected to be highly competitive and you are competing
against other projects.
