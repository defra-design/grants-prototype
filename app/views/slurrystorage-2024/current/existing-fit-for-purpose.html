Eligibility

To cover an existing store, you must confirm at full application that the store is structurally suitable to cover and meet regulations and build standards.

When is a store no longer fit for purpose?
A store is no longer fit for purpose if it has reached the end of its design life and may be at risk of leaks or failure.
