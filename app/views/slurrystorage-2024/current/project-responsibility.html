Will you take full responsibility for your project?
If you are on a short tenancy, you can ask your landlord to underwrite your agreement. This means they will take over your agreement if your tenancy ends. For example, your landlord could pass the agreed project to the new tenant.

This approach is optional and we will only ask for details at full application.

Eligibility

You must complete your project and keep the grant-funded items fit for purpose for 5 years after the date your receive your final grant payment.
