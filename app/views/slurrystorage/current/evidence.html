Evidence

If your project is invited to submit a full application, you need:

  • a storage calculation plan, which you will need to submit for review to the Environment Agency
  • confirmation that your project will comply with Water Resources (Control of Pollution) (Silage, Slurry and Agricultural Fuel Oil) regualations (SSAFO) and build standards (signed by you and your engineer)
  • a nutrient management plan
  • a declaration that you will comply with monitoring requirements
