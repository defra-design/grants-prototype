Eligibility

You must have a permit from the Environment Agency (EA) to rear pigs intensively in an installation if you have more than:

  • 2,000 places for production pigs (over 30kg)
  • 750 places for sows

You may need to apply for a change to your permit if you increase your slurry storage.
