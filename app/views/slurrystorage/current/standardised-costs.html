Grant amounts for eligible items

The Slurry Infrastructure grant uses standardised costs for the eligible items. The grant is 50% of the standardised cost.

At full application stage, you can choose between the standard grant for an item or you can submit 3 quotes.

If you submit quotes, your grant amount is based on 50% of the cheapest quote.
